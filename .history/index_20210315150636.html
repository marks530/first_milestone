What is an ICC Profile?

We can create an ICC profile for you remotely or we can come to your location any where in the country

An ICC Profile controls the color management of your printer inks. A color profile is designed to get the best quality prints from your printer – it changes the preset colours of your printer ink. For example, if your printer ink currently prints skin tones in a reddish colour, a printer profile from us will make the skin tone reflect the exact skin tone colour of the person.

We create custom made icc color profiles for your printer – all you need to do is print the colour chart on your ET-2700 sublimation ink using the instructions on our website – do this using the ink type and paper type you want an ICC profile for – then we will use advanced color calibration software to create a printer profile for your Epson ET-2700 sublimation ink. We will then email this to you, and when printing your documents you will set your printer settings to this printer profile so you gain the highest quality prints you can.
