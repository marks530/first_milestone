About Us

Print Better was founded on the promise of a complete ICC profiling service. We supply on time, provide a reliable service when you need it, and configure workflow systems that work. The company was formed in 1999 by Bob Jeffreys, Sean Whelan and Joe Power, three pre-press engineers, all having previously worked together with Agfa. The combined pre-press and print knowledge and experience of the founders has allowed the company to grow and prosper since then. Further appointments followed which included Les Sharpe and Noel Spaine, both with extensive experience in the industry. The company provides a high level of service and has established relationships with all the main printer manufacturers Our engineers are located in Dublin, Limerick and Roscommon. These locations have allowed for excellent nationwide coverage, meaning that calls are responded to quickly and efficiently. Parts, if needed, are shipped nationwide overnight and consumable items are delivered within 24 hours, and often less. We ensure that our staff always have the most up to date Colour Calibration software and the latest Spectrophotometers available on the market. Print Better is a complete provider colour workflows to large and small commercial printers and also caters for home office users, from a single profile to many profiles matching different makes of printers.
