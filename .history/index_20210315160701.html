What is an ICC Profile?

An ICC Profile controls the color management of your printer. A color profile is designed to get the best quality prints from your printer – it changes the preset colours of your printer ink. For example, if your printer currently prints skin tones in a reddish colour, a printer profile from us will make the skin tone reflect the exact skin tone colour of the person.
